Chapter 94: The Three-Day Jubilee

发布时间：2026/7/2 02:04:18

第九十四章：三日狂欢节

The house was transformed. Streamers hung from the ceiling, and the smell of expensive catering—far superior to any home-cooked meal—filled the air. The "Fujiwara Wish Fulfillment Party" was officially underway. In a grand gesture of gratitude for his "successful transaction," Fujiwara had signed an iron-clad commitment: he was the sole custodian of all domestic chores for the next six months.

这栋房子彻底变了样。天花板上挂着彩带，空气中弥漫着高档外卖的香气——远胜于任何家常便饭。“藤原愿望达成派对”正式开始了。为了感谢小百合的“成功撮合”，藤原签署了一份铁一般的承诺书：未来半年，全家的家务全包了。

Miyetsuko stood by the punch bowl, raising a glass to the neighbors. "It’s a victory for the whole house! Our domestic lead has secured his dream, and in exchange, our operational efficiency regarding laundry and dishes has improved by 100%!"

美悦子站在果汁盆旁，向邻居们举杯。“这是全家的胜利！我们的家务主管不仅达成了夙愿，还让我们的洗碗和洗衣效率提升了100%！”

Neighbor Wang Po, still snacking on her favorite sunflower seeds, leaned over to Rin. "So, did the 'legend' live up to the hype? Was she worth the wait?"

王婆还在嗑瓜子，凑到铃耳边八卦道：“怎么样？那传说中的‘女神’名副其实吗？值不值得等？”

Rin checked her nails, unfazed. "Let’s just say Fujiwara is exhausted. He’s been ‘researching’ for three hours straight. If he does any more ‘fieldwork,’ he’ll need a wheelchair for the rest of the week."

铃毫不在意地修着指甲。“这么说吧，藤原已经虚脱了。他连续‘科研’了三个小时。如果他再敢搞什么‘实地考察’，这周接下来的日子他得坐轮椅了。”

Sayuri walked by, wearing a smug smile. She patted Fujiwara on the shoulder as he struggled to carry a tray of drinks. "Keep it up, honey! You’ve got to earn that ‘Legendary’ status. By the way, the vacuum cleaner is broken again. Fix it after you finish serving the neighbors."

小百合一脸得意地走过，拍了拍正在吃力地搬饮料托盘的藤原。“加油，亲爱的！你得配得上‘传奇’这个名头。对了，吸尘器又坏了，伺候完邻居记得去修。”

Fujiwara wiped the sweat from his brow, clutching the tray like a lifeline. "I wanted a fantasy," he muttered, "but I seem to have just signed up for a lifetime of service."

藤原擦了擦额头的汗，死死抓着托盘，仿佛那是他最后的救命稻草。“我想要的是一场幻梦，”他嘟囔道，“结果好像只签下了一份终身劳务合同。”

Seiko, sitting in her usual corner, watched the chaos with a faint, knowing smirk. She turned to a neighbor who looked confused by the entire scene. "Don't look so shocked," she murmured. "In this house, even a miracle comes with a chore list."

圣子坐在角落里，带着一抹意味深长的浅笑注视着这场混乱。她看向旁边那个被眼前景象惊呆的邻居，低声说：“别那么吃惊。在这栋屋子里，哪怕是奇迹，背后也得带着一张家务清单。”

[Learning Point]

The Cost of Desire: 欲望的代价，藤原通过“牺牲”换取了“偶像”，而这种牺牲立刻被家庭系统转化为高强度的劳动力剥削。

The Normalization of Taboo: 禁忌的常态化，邻居们的参与将这种混乱的家庭关系推向了一种诡异的“社区奇观”。

Humor as Deflection: 幽默作为防御，通过自嘲和调侃，她们消解了家庭内部可能存在的权力斗争，维持了表面的和谐。
