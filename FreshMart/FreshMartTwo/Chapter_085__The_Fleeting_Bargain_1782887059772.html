Chapter 85: The Fleeting Bargain

发布时间：2026/7/1 14:24:18

第八十五章：短暂的交易

Sayuri grabbed a handful of sunflower seeds from a decorative bowl and pressed them into Wang Po’s hand. Her tone was sharp, a practiced dismissal masked by a thin veil of hospitality. "Here, Auntie, take these and go find a cool spot to rest—or perhaps somewhere else to stir up trouble."

小百合抓了一把瓜子塞进王婆手里，语气犀利，用一层薄薄的虚假客气掩盖着赶人的意图。“大娘，这些拿去吃吧，哪凉快哪玩去吧！”

To everyone's surprise, Wang Po didn't bristle. Instead, she settled back into the sofa, her face breaking into a wide, toothy grin. She began to crack the seeds with expert precision, spitting the shells onto the pristine hardwood floor with reckless abandon. She seemed delighted, as if she had just landed a major contract. Sayuri watched the litter scatter, her resistance finally wearing thin. She sighed, her bravado folding under the sheer persistence of the older woman. "Fine," Sayuri relented, her voice trailing off. "I’ll go meet your nephew in a few days. But only to look. If... if he happens to be well-endowed enough, I might consider it—strictly with Fujiwara’s permission, of course—to be his 'three-day wife.' No more."

出乎所有人的意料，王婆并没有生气，反而一屁股陷进沙发里，露出了满脸褶子的得意笑容。她熟练地嗑着瓜子，将壳随手丢在光洁的地板上，笑得合不拢嘴。小百合见状无奈地摇了摇头，在那份执拗面前败下阵来，轻叹一口气：“行吧，我答应你。过几天我去见见你那侄儿。不过先说好，只是去看看。要是他那玩意儿确实够大……那我倒可以考虑在得到藤原许可的前提下，跟他做个三日夫妻。多了免谈。”

Rin, who had been silent, suddenly perked up, her eyes bright with a strange, impulsive curiosity. "Wait," she interjected. "Let her go first, and if he’s actually any good... then keep me in mind. I’m tired of the same old walls. If he’s truly impressive, I might even consider going to the civil affairs bureau with him and actually registering."

原本沉默的铃突然抬起头，眼中闪过一丝奇异的冲动与好奇。“等等，”她插话道，“让姐姐先去。要是他真有本事……那也给我留个位置。我腻味了总是看着一样的墙壁。如果他真有那么出众，我甚至可以考虑直接跟他去领证登记。”

Miyetsuko finally set her pen down. The silence that followed was absolute. She leaned back, her gaze distant, as if calculating the long-term utility of such a liaison. "If he is as remarkable as you claim, Wang Po, don't leave me out of the ledger. I have a gap in my schedule after the next business cycle closes. I wouldn't mind testing the 'equipment' myself. We’ll talk then."

美悦子终于放下了手中的钢笔，房间里顿时陷入一片死寂。她向后靠在椅背上，眼神深邃，仿佛在评估这段关系的长期资产价值。“王婆，如果他真如你所说的那样出类拔萃，那账本上也得给我留个位子。下个业务周期结束后，我有个空档。我不介意亲自去验验那套‘设备’。到时候再说吧。”

Wang Po continued to crunch on the seeds, her laughter echoing through the room—a sound of triumph. She looked at the three women, now all seemingly vying for a taste of her mysterious nephew, and then her eyes drifted back to Seiko, who remained quiet in the corner. "And the little one?" Wang Po asked, tapping her chin. "Does she get a turn, or is she still too fragile for the market?"

王婆继续欢快地磕着瓜子，得意的笑声回荡在客厅里。她看着眼前这三个似乎都在争抢着要“尝鲜”的女人，最后将目光又一次投向了角落里安静的圣子。“那小丫头呢？”王婆指尖敲着下巴问道，“她也轮得上吗？还是说，她还没到能进这市场的年纪？”

The Normalization of Excess: 过度的常态化，从最初的“包养合同”到如今“排队领证的博弈”，她们将人性中最私密的婚姻关系彻底降格为一种可交易的轮转资产。

The Strategic Shift: 战略性转向，三人态度的转变展现了她们对于“新鲜感”与“资源交换”的渴求，婚姻不再是束缚，而成了逃离当下枯燥生活的手段。

The Marketization of Seiko: 对圣子的市场化，王婆的追问暗示了圣子已被视为某种待价而沽的潜在资源，彻底打破了家庭最后的避风港。
