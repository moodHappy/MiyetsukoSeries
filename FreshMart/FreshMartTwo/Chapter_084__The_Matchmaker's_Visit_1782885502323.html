Chapter 84: The Matchmaker's Visit

发布时间：2026/7/1 13:58:19

第八十四章：王婆的算盘

The house had barely recovered from the hospital scare before another distraction knocked at the door. Neighbor Wang Po, a woman whose nose for business was only outmatched by her appetite for gossip, had arrived with a mission. She settled herself comfortably in the living room, fanning herself with a lace handkerchief, her eyes darting between the three women like a merchant appraising fine silk.

房子还没从医院的惊吓中完全缓过神来，另一桩琐事便叩响了门扉。邻居王婆，一个商业嗅觉远胜于八卦热情的女人，带着使命登门了。她在客厅里安顿下来，手里摇着蕾丝手帕，目光像商人品鉴绸缎一样，在三个女人之间游移。

"Rin, darling," Wang Po began, leaning in with a conspiratorial smirk. "I have a nephew, a real catch. Steady work, owns his own shop. Surely you aren't planning to spend the rest of your days lingering in the shadows of this... arrangement? You’re young, beautiful."

“铃啊，亲爱的，”王婆开了口，带着一副心照不宣的坏笑，“我有个侄子，那是真的不错。工作稳定，还有自己的店。你难道真打算余生都耗在这个……这种安排的阴影里吗？你还年轻，又漂亮。”

Rin didn't even look up from her tea. Her expression was serene, yet immovable. "You’re too kind, Auntie Wang, but my heart is already spoken for. I belong to Fujiwara, body and soul. I have no intention of marrying another; I prefer to keep myself pure and devoted to him." Her words were soft, yet they carried a finality that brooked no debate.

铃连头都没抬，手中的茶杯平稳如初，神情宁静却不可动摇。“王婆您太客气了，但我心有所属。我已经是藤原的人了，身心皆然。我没有再嫁的打算，只想守着这份心意，保持清白。”她的话语轻柔，却带着一股不容反驳的坚定。

Wang Po’s smile faltered for a fraction of a second before she pivoted toward Sayuri. "Well, then! And you, Sayuri? Such a vibrant girl, surely you’d want a proper home of your own?"

王婆的笑容僵硬了一瞬，随即将话锋转向小百合。“那好吧！你呢，小百合？这么活泼的姑娘，难道不想有个正儿八经的家吗？”

Sayuri chuckled, her voice as light as a melody. "Oh, Auntie, I’m afraid my schedule is quite tied up. I’m currently under a comprehensive 'maintenance contract' with Fujiwara. My time, my services, and my presence are all fully occupied. There’s simply no legal room for a husband, even if I wanted one."

小百合轻声一笑，语调轻快如歌。“哎呀，大婶，恐怕我的日程排得太满了。我目前在与藤原的包养合同之内，我的时间、服务乃至我的存在本身都已经全额锁定。就算我想，也没有法律空间再容纳一位丈夫了。”

Wang Po sighed, her gaze finally landing on Miyetsuko. The matriarch of the household barely glanced up from her ledger, her pen scratching rhythmically against the paper. "Miyetsuko, surely you see the wisdom in—"

王婆叹了口气，目光最终落在了美悦子身上。这位屋里的女主人连头都没抬，正对着账本忙碌，钢笔在纸上发出有节奏的沙沙声。“美悦子，难道你看不出……”

"I am Fujiwara’s only legal wife," Miyetsuko interrupted, her voice cool and authoritative. "While I wouldn't be averse to the thrill of a wedding—it is, after all, quite the spectacle—my business interests currently demand my full attention. We can revisit the prospect of me taking another husband when the quarterly reports are closed. For now, I'm simply too busy."

“我是藤原法律上唯一的合法妻子，”美悦子打断了她，声音清冷而威严。“虽然我不介意再体验一次婚礼的快感——毕竟那确实是场盛大的戏码——但目前的生意让我分身乏术。等季度报表结清了，再谈我二婚的事吧。现在，我实在是太忙了。”

Wang Po blinked, stunned by the cold efficiency of the refusal. Frustrated but not defeated, she turned her eyes toward Seiko, who was sitting quietly in the corner, nursing a cup of broth and looking remarkably fragile. "And... what about her?" Wang Po whispered, gesturing toward the younger girl. "Sayuri, what’s her story?"

王婆眨了眨眼，被这冷酷且高效的拒绝方式震住了。她感到沮丧却并未罢休，转而看向正缩在角落里、小口喝着热汤且看起来格外脆弱的圣子。“那……她呢？”王婆压低声音，指了指那个年轻女孩，“小百合，她是什么情况？”

[Learning Point]

The "Domesticated" Commodity: “家养”的商品，通过这种拒绝的方式，三人展示了她们虽然地位不同，但都已成为了藤原这一“私有资产”的一部分，且对此表现出了一种扭曲的自豪感。

The Business of Marriage: 婚姻的商业化，美悦子将“再婚”视为一项需要排期处理的商业项目，彻底消解了婚姻的神圣性。

The Outsider's Confusion: 外来者的困惑，王婆作为传统秩序的代表，无法理解这栋屋子里的人如何将“被包养”视为一种不可侵犯的身份。
