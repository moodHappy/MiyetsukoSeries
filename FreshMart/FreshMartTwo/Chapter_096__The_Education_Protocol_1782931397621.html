Chapter 96: The Education Protocol

发布时间：2026/7/2 02:43:15

第九十六章：教育协议

The house was quiet, save for the hum of the refrigerator. Seiko sat in the dim light of the living room, completely absorbed in a comic book. Sayuri, parched from her long day on set, emerged from her room to get a glass of water. As she passed Seiko, she glanced down and froze. It was a graphic, explicit hentai manga. With a sharp exhale of disapproval, she snatched the book away, leaving Seiko stunned and empty-handed.

屋子里静悄悄的，只有冰箱发出的低沉嗡嗡声。圣子坐在客厅昏暗的角落里，正全神贯注地看着一本漫画书。小百合拍了一天戏，口渴难耐，出来倒水时瞥了一眼，整个人僵住了。那是一本大尺度的成人漫画。她轻哼一声表示不满，一把夺过了书，留下圣子一脸错愕。

That evening, the family gathered around the mahogany dining table. It wasn't dinner—it was a board meeting.

当晚，家人们聚集在红木餐桌旁。这不再是晚餐时间——这更像是一场董事会。

"We have a problem," Sayuri began, tossing the confiscated manga onto the table like a piece of hazardous material. "Seiko is reading this. I don't think it’s appropriate."

“有个问题，”小百合开头道，把那本没收的漫画像处理危险品一样扔在桌上，“圣子在看这个，我觉得非常不合适。”

Miyetsuko adjusted her glasses, her gaze razor-sharp. "Appropriate? That’s subjective. In this house, we treat everything as an asset or a lesson. Is it educational, or is it a distraction?"

美悦子扶了扶眼镜，目光如刀。“合适？这太主观了。在这个家里，所有东西要么是资产，要么是教材。这东西是有教育意义，还是纯粹的干扰？”

Rin leaned back, twirling a lock of hair. "She’s growing up. Maybe she’s just curious about what we do all day. If she’s going to be part of this family, she needs to understand the mechanics of it sooner or later."

铃靠在椅背上，玩弄着一缕头发。“她长大了。也许她只是好奇我们每天在干什么。如果她以后要成为这个家庭的一部分，迟早得理解这些‘运作机制’。”

Fujiwara looked uneasy, his eyes darting to Seiko, who sat silent and observant. "She’s just a kid. There’s a line between 'understanding' and... whatever we have going on here. I don't want to rush her innocence."

藤原感到局促不安，眼神扫向沉默且观察着一切的圣子。“她还是个孩子。‘理解’和……我们现在搞的这些名堂之间是有界限的。我不想太快摧毁她的纯真。”

"Innocence is a luxury we don't have," Miyetsuko countered coldly. "If she’s going to live here, she needs to be prepared for the reality of our environment. But it shouldn't be learned from a cheap comic. It needs a curriculum."

“纯真是我们消费不起的奢侈品，”美悦子冷冷反驳，“如果她要住在这里，就必须为这种环境下的现实做好准备。但这些知识不该从廉价漫画里学，我们需要一套课程体系。”

Seiko finally spoke, her voice calm and chillingly mature. "I was just looking for a reference. You all treat sex like a business plan—why shouldn't I?"

圣子终于开口了，声音冷静且透着一种令人胆寒的成熟。“我只是在找参考资料。你们把性当成商业计划书来运作——那我为什么不能呢？”

The room went silent. Miyetsuko let out a single, approving nod. "A curriculum it is, then. We start tomorrow."

房间里陷入死寂。美悦子点了点头，表示赞许。“那就定课程表吧。明天开始。”

[Learning Point]

The Normalization of the Abnormal: 异常行为的常态化，当所有家庭成员都将性视为一种“业务”或“工具”时，对未成年人的“性教育”也就变成了一种职前培训。

The Absence of Boundaries: 界限的缺失，藤原试图保护“纯真”的尝试在美悦子的“资产经营”逻辑面前显得毫无抵抗力。

Curriculum of Cynicism: 玩世不恭的课程，将本来属于个人成长的探索转化为冷酷的“业务培训”，是这个家庭彻底去道德化的标志。
