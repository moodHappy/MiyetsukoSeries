Chapter 82: The Joyous Reunion

发布时间：2026/7/1 11:28:31

第八十二章：欢乐的重逢

Before Miyetsuko could even initiate the complex web of back-channel maneuvers and payments she had been planning, the prison system delivered an unexpected surprise. Due to a clerical review and a sudden mark of "exemplary behavior" in her file, Rin was released—nearly half a day earlier than her sentence required. She stepped out into the cool evening air, the heavy iron gates clanging shut behind her, signaling the end of her nightmare.

美悦子还没来得及动用她精心筹划的那些幕后关系网，监狱系统就给了一个意外的惊喜。由于文书复核和档案中突然标注的“表现良好”，铃被提前释放了——比预期的刑期早了近半天。她跨出大门，呼吸着傍晚清凉的空气，身后那沉重的铁门轰然关上，宣告了她那段梦魇的终结。

That night, the family reunited at the city’s most opulent hotel. The lobby glittered with gold leaf and crystal chandeliers, a stark, breathtaking contrast to the grime of the cell block. As they entered the private dining suite, the tension that had gripped them for months finally broke. Fujiwara embraced Rin, his hold tight and protective, while Sayuri laughed, her eyes wet with relief, and Miyetsuko toasted their survival with a glass of vintage champagne.

那天晚上，一家人在市内最豪华的酒店重逢。大堂里金碧辉煌，水晶吊灯璀璨夺目，与牢房的污秽形成了令人窒息的强烈对比。当他们踏入私人包间时，那股困扰了他们数月的紧绷感终于断裂。藤原拥抱着铃，双臂紧绷，带着保护者的姿态；小百合笑着，眼中闪烁着如释重负的泪光；美悦子则举起一杯陈年香槟，为他们的劫后余生致敬。

Rin, surprisingly, was the life of the party. The trauma of the cage seemed to have been buried under a veneer of reckless, dark humor. Over the second course, she began to recount her experiences with a mischievous, unapologetic sparkle in her eyes. She told them a ribald story about a clumsy inmate who had tried to take advantage of her during laundry detail—a man whose pride was as massive as his anatomy was underwhelming.

铃出人意料地成为了宴会的主角。牢笼带来的阴影似乎被一层鲁莽而阴郁的幽默感所掩盖。在吃到第二道菜时，她眼中闪烁着顽皮而无所顾忌的光芒，开始讲述她在狱中的遭遇。她绘声绘色地讲了一个关于洗衣房里的笨拙囚犯的荤段子——那个男人自视甚高，可惜那方面的本钱却实在寒碜。

"He was so confident," Rin giggled, mimicking the man’s swagger to the delight of the table. "But when he finally tried to force himself in, it was like throwing a toothpick down a hallway. He pushed and pushed, but he couldn't even manage to find his way inside me! I just laid there, laughing until he got so frustrated he gave up."

“他当时那叫一个自信满满，”铃咯咯笑着，模仿着那男人的狂妄姿态，逗得全桌人忍俊不禁。“可当他真想硬来的时候，那感觉就像把一根牙签往走廊里扔。他进进出出折腾半天，甚至连我的穴口都找不到！我就那么躺着，一直笑，笑到他挫败得直接放弃了。”

The room erupted. Miyetsuko laughed until her sides ached, Sayuri clapped her hands, and even Fujiwara’s stoic expression cracked into a genuine, relieved smile. It was a moment of profound, liberating catharsis. In the heart of the most luxurious suite in the city, surrounded by crystal and silk, they were finally, truly whole again.

房间里爆发出一阵大笑。美悦子笑得花枝乱颤，小百合拍着手掌，连藤原那张严肃的脸也裂开了一抹真诚而欣慰的笑容。这是一场深刻而彻底的宣泄。在这座城市最豪华的包间中央，被水晶与丝绸环绕，他们终于，真正地完整了。

[Learning Point]

Gallows Humor: 绞刑架幽默，铃通过讲述关于自己被侵犯经历的荤段子，完成了对心理创伤的消解与掌控。

Reintegration: 重归社会，这种庆祝仪式不仅是生理上的团聚，更是对家庭地位与心理状态的“重置”。

The Victory of Absurdity: 荒谬的胜利，当他们能用欢笑对抗那段黑暗经历时，便意味着体制的惩罚在他们的人性面前彻底失效。
