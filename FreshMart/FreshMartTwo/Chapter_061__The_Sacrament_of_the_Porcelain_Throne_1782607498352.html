Chapter 61: The Sacrament of the Porcelain Throne

发布时间：2026/6/28 08:44:52

第六十一章：陶瓷王座的圣餐

The warehouse was suffused with the pungent, sweet stench of fermenting human waste and cheap incense. In the center, sitting atop the "Holy Toilet," sat the Toilet Saint. He wasn't preaching; he was simply gurgling, his tiny finger pointing at a pile of discarded barcode scanners. Hundreds of desperate, hollow-eyed citizens knelt before him, chanting in rhythmic, gasping wheezes.

仓库里弥漫着发酵的人类排泄物与廉价香火混合的甜腥味。在那只被称为“圣座”的马桶上，马桶圣子正瘫在那儿，他没有布道，只是发出咕噜声，小手指向一堆废弃的条形码扫描枪。成百上千双空洞的眼睛跪拜在他脚下，机械地念诵着某种干涩的祈祷词。

Suddenly, the steel doors buckled. Tactical units poured in, black armor glistening under the harsh searchlights. "Federal Security!" the lead officer bellowed, his voice distorted by a gas mask. "You are all under arrest for sedition, subversion, and the unauthorized veneration of sanitation hardware!"

突然，钢门轰然炸裂。全副武装的特警蜂拥而入，黑色护甲在探照灯下泛着油光。“联邦安全局！”领头者隔着防毒面具嘶吼，“你们因涉嫌颠覆国家政权、非法崇拜卫浴硬件被逮捕了！”

Fujiwara, whose bravery was inversely proportional to his blood alcohol level, didn't hesitate. He dived under the makeshift altar, curling into a ball like a frightened hedgehog, shivering so hard his teeth rattled against the concrete. "I’m just here for the snacks!" he whimpered to a pile of cigarette butts. "I don’t know anything about the plumbing revolution!"

藤原的勇气与血液里的酒精浓度成反比，他没有任何犹豫，像只受惊的刺猬一样钻进简易祭坛下面，抖得牙齿都在敲击混凝土。“我只是来蹭零食的！”他对着一堆烟头哀鸣，“我对马桶革命一无所知！”

Sayuri saw an opportunity. She didn't cower; she slithered toward the lead officer, her lace veil fluttering like a moth’s wing. She caught his visor with a suggestive, lingering gaze.

小百合没躲，她像条蛇一样滑向领头的特警，蕾丝面纱如蛾翅般颤动。她用一种带有暗示性的、黏腻的眼神死死盯着他的护目镜。

"Officer," she purred, her voice dripping with the practiced, hypnotic cadence of a legendary screen siren. "Do you really want to process all this paperwork? It’s so... abrasive. Why don't we retire to the back room? I’m sure my performance review will convince you that this entire cult is just... a misunderstanding."

“警官，”她柔声说，声音里带着影星惯有的催眠节奏，“你真想处理这些繁琐的文书吗？多……伤皮肤啊。不如我们去后室谈谈？我相信我的‘表演评估’能让你相信，这所谓的邪教只是一场……误会。”

"And if that’s not enough," Rin added, stepping forward with her industrial scanner. She began to 'beep' rhythmically near the officer's groin. "I can provide a deep-tissue diagnostic massage. I charge by the kilojoule."

“如果这还不够，”铃走上前，挥舞着工业扫描枪在警官胯部附近发出规律的滴滴声，“我可以提供深层组织诊断按摩。按千焦耳收费。”

The tactical team hesitated, their weapons wavering. The absurdity was physically overwhelming; the air was thick with the smell of the Saint, and the sight of a famous ex-actress trying to seduce a SWAT leader while a girl scanned his crotch was, objectively, a tactical nightmare.

特警们犹豫了，枪口开始晃动。这种荒谬感带来的物理压迫力实在太强——空气里全是圣子的臭味，而一个影星正试图勾引SWAT队长，旁边还有个女孩在扫描他的胯下，这简直是战术层面的噩梦。

Miyetsuko stepped forward, completely unbothered. She clapped her hands, and the room fell silent.

美悦子优雅地走上前，完全不受影响。她拍了拍手，全场鸦雀无声。

"Gentlemen," she said, her voice dripping with executive authority. "Do you know what's in that box?" She pointed to a crate of her latest product: the 'First Love' PVC sewage-paste dispensers. "It’s free. A corporate gift to the state. You take these crates, you leave, and you tell your superiors you broke up an insurrection. You get the credit, we get to continue our religious observation, and the public gets a taste of... nostalgia."

“绅士们，”她说，声音里带着CEO般的独断，“你们知道那箱子里装的是什么吗？”她指向那一箱最新的“初恋”PVC污水膏。“那是免费的。给国家的赠礼。你们带走这些箱子，离开这儿，回去告诉上司你们镇压了一场起义。你们赚了功勋，我们继续礼拜，而公众能尝到那股……‘怀旧’的味道。”

The officer looked at the boxes, then at the shivering Fujiwara under the table, then back at Sayuri, who was currently unzipping the officer's tactical vest.

警官看看箱子，看看桌下抖如筛糠的藤原，又看看正试图拉开他战术背心拉链的小百合。

"It’s industrial sludge, isn't it?" the officer asked, his voice cracking.

“那是工业污泥，对吧？”警官的声音带上了哭腔。

"It's 'Authentic Experience'," Miyetsuko corrected him. "Take the crates. Don't look behind the curtain. And for God’s sake, keep the receipts."

“是‘真实体验’，”美悦子纠正他，“带上箱子，别往幕后看。看在上帝份上，收好发票。”

The officers scrambled to grab the crates, retreating with the frantic haste of men who had seen too much. As the steel doors slammed shut, the "Toilet Saint" gurgled once, loudly, and the congregation returned to their silent, shivering prayer.

特警们手忙脚乱地扛起箱子，逃跑的姿态仿佛一群窥见了禁忌真相的落难者。随着钢门再次轰然关闭，“马桶圣子”响亮地咕噜了一声，信众们重新陷入了那沉默而颤栗的祈祷中。
