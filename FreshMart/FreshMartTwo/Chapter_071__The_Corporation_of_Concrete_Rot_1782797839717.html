Chapter 71: The Corporation of Concrete Rot

发布时间：2026/6/30 13:37:17

第七十一章：水泥腐烂的公司

The picturesque guest house, located between a failing golf course and a rusted gas station, served as the cornerstone of their latest incarnation. Inside the drawing room, the furnishings were exceedingly homogeneous, smelling of damp dust and respiratory failure. Fujiwara sat on the floor, nursing his hypertension, while Miyetsuko, acting as the headmistress of this absurd asylum, obsessed over the paradoxical materialism of their predicament.

这座坐落在破败高尔夫球场与锈迹斑斑的加油站之间的如画旅馆，成了他们最新化身的基石。绘图室内的陈设极其均质，散发着潮湿的灰尘和呼吸衰竭的味道。藤原坐在地板上，照料着自己的高血压，而美悦子——这座荒谬收容所的女校长，正痴迷于他们困境中那悖论式的唯物主义。

"We are fund raising for a counterfeit future," Miyetsuko declared, her eccentricity emphatically displayed. "It’s problematic, yes, but principally pleasurable to impersonate the front runner of a dying society." She was disseminating informative yet detrimental qualitative data through a hacked switchboard that hummed like a synthesizer. Rin, stationed at the console, was precipitate-ing a convergence of hypothetical systems. "The progression of our entitlement," Rin muttered, "is a correlation between empowerment and the rule of law—which, currently, is an obstruction to our survival."

“我们正在为虚假的未来筹款，”美悦子宣布，她那怪癖被强调得淋漓尽致。“这确实有问题，但扮演一个垂死社会的领跑者，原则上还是挺愉快的。”她正通过一台嗡嗡作响如合成器般的破解交换机，散布着既具启发性又有害的定性数据。铃守在控制台前，正催化着假设系统的汇聚。“我们权利的进程，”铃嘟囔道，“是赋权与法治之间的相关性——而目前，法治反而是我们生存的阻碍。”

Sayuri, attempting to resuscitate her career, was acting as the interviewee for a hypocritical evangelical talk show. Her promiscuous behavior at the local observatory had made her a problematic figure, but she remained disciplined in her descriptive lies. "The designation of our class," she told the void, "is hierarchical, patriarchal, and exceedingly pessimistic." She was marginalizing her own trauma, an impractical formulation of the egalitarian dream.

小百合为了复兴事业，正作为一名伪善福音派脱口秀的受访者出镜。她在当地天文台的放荡行为让她成为一个颇具争议的人物，但她依然在描述性的谎言中保持着纪律。“我们阶级的定性，”她对着虚空说道，“是等级森严、父权至上且极其悲观的。”她正在边缘化自己的创伤，那是一个关于平等主义梦想的不切实际的构想。

"We necessitate an enhancement of the lie," Miyetsuko shouted, her intolerance for reality far reaching. "The determinant of our incongruous existence is not a hand grenade—it is the presumption of order." She interrogated Fujiwara about his pessimistic disapproval of the plan.

“我们需要加强谎言，”美悦子尖叫道，她对现实的不耐烦影响深远。“我们不协调生存的决定因素不是手榴弹——而是对秩序的假定。”她质问藤原为何对计划持悲观的反对态度。

Fujiwara wiped his brow. "The correlation between our contractual misery and the prohibitive cost of survival is the determinant factor here. We are in house arrest within our own madness, waiting for the respiratory system of this world to snap."

藤原擦了擦额头。“我们合同约定的苦难与生存的高昂成本之间的相关性，才是这里的决定因素。我们正处于自身疯狂的软禁之中，等待着这个世界的呼吸系统断裂。”

Outside, the picturesque view of the observatory remained cold. They were caught in a qualitative predicament where only a hand grenade of logic—or perhaps a refrigerate-d synthesizer—could offer a final enhancement to their hypothetical progression.

窗外，天文台那如画的景色依旧冰冷。他们陷入了一种定性的困境，只有逻辑的手榴弹——或者说一台冷藏的合成器——能为他们假设性的进程提供最后的强化。
