第七十八章：量子停车场

The ongoing decay of the mainland had driven them to a Georgian-style car park in the Midlands, situated precariously near a polluted woodland. Fujiwara, acting as a dubious salesman and chronic borrower, sat on the hood of an armoured trailer. He was reading a lengthy booklet on uranium farming and calcium-rich compost. "To analyse the equation of our survival," he told an athletic Hispanic drummer who was passing by, "is to head on confront a syndrome of Fascist bureaucracy. We must minimize our expectations of this aviation-grade disaster."

大陆持续的衰败将他们赶到了米德兰兹一个乔治亚风格的停车场，它危险地坐落在一片被污染的林地旁。藤原，作为一个可疑的推销员和长期的借款人，坐在一辆装甲拖车的引擎盖上。他正读着一本关于铀农业和富含钙的堆肥的冗长小册子。“要分析我们生存的方程式，”他对路过的一位体格健壮的西班牙裔鼓手说，“就是迎头痛击法西斯官僚主义的综合症。我们必须尽量降低对这场航空级灾难的期望。”

Miyetsuko, the notable reformer and self-appointed trustee of their sketchy dealings, was trying to compile a lawsuit against a retailer and supplier of a failed vaccine. "We must bolster our advisory board and exclude the corrupt lawmakers," she declared, her glamour and stylish presence serving as a lead in to a fiery Marxist rant. "We will boycott the local fishery and thereby inflict an atrocity upon the remaining royalty. The equality of this epidemic is a broadly unclear spectrum, whereby we are ultimately forced to emigrate."

美悦子，这位著名的改革者以及他们那些可疑交易自封的受托人，正试图针对一家失败疫苗的零售商和供应商编纂一场诉讼。“我们必须加强我们的顾问委员会，并将腐败的立法者排除在外，”她宣布，她那充满魅力与时尚感的存在，成了她激烈马克思主义怒斥的开场白。“我们将抵制当地的渔业，从而对残存的皇室施加暴行。这场流行病的平等性是一个广泛而不清晰的光谱，借此我们最终将被迫移民。”

Sayuri, desperately trying to maintain her status as a gorgeous heroine, was attempting to endorse a topical rainbow-colored parsley starter at a makeshift premiere. She felt like a dinosaur breeder in a world defined by quantum hydrogen. "The costing of this booking is absurd," she complained to an admiral from a rogue military brigade. "My allergy to this fertile Oriental-themed dirt makes me a sufferer of a durable molecule." She seemed to alienate everyone amongst the crowd, ready to quarrel over a dead composer's obituary rather than face reality.

小百合，拼命想维持她那华丽女主角的地位，正试图在一场临时首映式上代言一道色彩斑斓、撒着彩虹色欧芹的热门前菜。在被量子氢定义的世界里，她觉得自己就像个恐龙饲养员。“这次预订的成本核算太荒谬了，”她向一名来自流氓军事旅的海军上将抱怨道，“我对这种肥沃的东方主题泥土过敏，这让我成了一个持久分子的受难者。”她似乎疏远了人群中的每一个人，宁愿为了一个死去的作曲家的讣告争吵，也不愿面对现实。

Rin, the creator and planner of their ramshackle tech, tried to disclose the accuracy of a new antibody while acting as a referee in a dispute over the custody of a rusted shuttle. She was ready to readily overturn even the Vatican's stance on airlift operations if it meant finding parts. "We must contend with the virus," she muttered, acting as a gardener of digital rot. "If we don't specify the parameters, the whole thing will pollute our systems."

铃，作为他们破烂技术的创造者和规划师，试图公开一种新抗体的准确性，同时还在一场关于生锈航天飞机监护权的争端中充当裁判。如果能找到零件，她随时准备推翻梵蒂冈对空运操作的立场。“我们必须与病毒抗争，”她嘟囔着，充当着数字腐烂的园丁，“如果我们不明确指定参数，整个东西都会污染我们的系统。”
